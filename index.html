Punchcode Bio

Erin Foster

Erin Foster is a creative, compassionate, and entrepreneurial, full-stack developer in the making. She is currently a student in Tech Impact’s, Punchcode: Full-stack Developer program, in Las Vegas, Nevada. Erin has always had a heart for community involvement and compassion projects. In the Fall of 2020, Erin founded “Peacefull Apparel” and “Heart Dreams Productions”, with the ultimate goal of cultivating creative outlets that uplift communities in need, around the world.

Erin’s community involvement with organizations like, “Food Not Bombs”, “LV Town Fridge”, “Litter Free NV”, and many others, inspired her to use her own creative passions of art, fashion, and music, to make a positive impact on one community at a time. After successfully completing the Punchcode program, Erin looks forward to channeling her many passions into the tech and software engineering industry.
